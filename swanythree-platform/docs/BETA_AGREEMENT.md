# SwanyThree Platform — Beta Agreement

**Effective Date**: Upon acceptance
**Platform**: SwanyThree Live Streaming Platform

---

## 1. Beta Program Overview

By participating in the SwanyThree Beta Program ("Beta"), you ("Participant") agree to the following terms and conditions. The Beta provides early access to the SwanyThree live streaming platform for testing, feedback, and content creation purposes.

## 2. Eligibility

- Must be 18 years of age or older
- Must provide a valid email address and create an account
- Must agree to this Beta Agreement and the SwanyThree Terms of Service

## 3. Platform Features (Beta)

The following features are available during the Beta period:

- **Live Streaming**: RTMP ingest with HLS delivery
- **20-Guest Video Panel**: WebRTC-based multi-guest streaming
- **Multi-Platform Restreaming**: Simultaneous broadcast to YouTube, Twitch, Kick, Facebook, and X
- **Tipping & Revenue**: 90/10 creator-favorable revenue split
- **Gamification**: XP, levels, streaks, badges, and challenges
- **Watch Parties**: Synchronized video playback
- **AI Features**: SwanyBot co-host and content moderation

## 4. Revenue Split

During the Beta period and beyond, SwanyThree guarantees:

- **90% to Creator**: Of net revenue after payment processor fees
- **10% to Platform**: Platform operational fee
- **Processor fees**: Deducted first (currently 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction via Stripe)
- This split is enforced at the database level and cannot be altered without a new agreement

## 5. Beta Participant Responsibilities

Participants agree to:

- Report bugs, issues, and feedback through designated channels
- Not attempt to exploit, reverse-engineer, or compromise platform security
- Not use the platform for illegal activities, harassment, or content violating community guidelines
- Maintain the confidentiality of unreleased features when requested

## 6. Content Guidelines

All content must comply with:

- No hate speech, harassment, or discrimination
- No illegal content or activities
- No explicit content without proper age-gating (when available)
- No impersonation of other individuals or organizations
- No spam, scams, or misleading content
- Respect for intellectual property rights

Violations may result in immediate account suspension.

## 7. Data & Privacy

- SwanyThree collects account information, usage data, and content metadata
- RTMP streaming keys are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plaintext)

- SwanyThree does not sell personal data to third parties
